Step 05 · Land cover change · 2021→2023

Land cover change & statistics

Most pixels were stable in 2021–2023 (clearest in the mountains). Crops was the class most generally seen as change in the 2023 land cover, throughout the country. The easternmost point experienced significant change, with strong growth of flooded vegetation.

Sources of gain and loss were not random: the first source of gain and first source of loss explained more than 50% of the change in most cases. Rangeland was the main source of both loss and gain for Trees (PM10) and Crops (PM2.5); for Built Area (NO₂), crops were the highest source of both loss and gain. Stability was high for every class — lowest for Built Area (95.17%).
